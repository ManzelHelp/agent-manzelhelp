## üéØ Executive Summary

**ManzelHelp** is a comprehensive service marketplace platform that connects customers who need help with skilled taskers (service providers) who can offer their expertise. The platform facilitates a two-way marketplace where:

- **Customers** can post jobs and book services directly from taskers
- **Taskers** can offer services and apply to customer job postings
- Both parties benefit from secure payments, verified profiles, and a trusted community

### Mission Statement
"Your All-in-One Service Solution - Connect, help, and grow together!"

### Value Proposition
- **For Customers**: Find trusted local helpers quickly and securely
- **For Taskers**: Offer your skills, earn extra income, and build your professional reputation
- **For Both**: Easy, friendly, and secure platform with quality assurance

## üíº Business Model

### Platform Type
**Two-Sided Marketplace** connecting service providers (Taskers) with service seekers (Customers)

### Revenue Streams
1. **Platform Fees**: Transaction fees on completed jobs/services
2. **Promotion Packages**: Premium visibility for taskers and companies
3. **Premium Applications**: Taskers can pay for priority application placement
4. **Job/Service Promotion**: Boost visibility of job postings and service offerings

### Business Model Components
- **B2C Marketplace**: Direct connection between customers and individual taskers
- **B2B Marketplace**: Companies can register and offer services through multiple taskers
- **Freemium Model**: Basic features free, premium features paid

## üîÑ Business Processes

### Customer Journey

#### 1. Registration & Onboarding
```
Sign Up ‚Üí Email Verification ‚Üí Complete Profile ‚Üí Choose Role (Customer/Tasker/Both)
```

#### 2. Posting a Job
```
Create Job Posting ‚Üí Add Details (description, budget, timeline, location) ‚Üí 
Upload Images ‚Üí Submit for Review ‚Üí Job Goes Live ‚Üí Receive Applications ‚Üí 
Review Applications ‚Üí Select Tasker ‚Üí Assign Job
```

#### 3. Booking a Service
```
Browse Services ‚Üí Search/Filter ‚Üí View Service Details ‚Üí View Tasker Profile ‚Üí 
Check Availability ‚Üí Book Service ‚Üí Confirm Booking ‚Üí Service Completed ‚Üí 
Payment Processed ‚Üí Leave Review
```

#### 4. Job Completion Flow
```
Job Assigned ‚Üí Tasker Starts Work ‚Üí Job In Progress ‚Üí Job Completed ‚Üí 
Customer Reviews ‚Üí Payment Released ‚Üí Review Posted
```

### Tasker Journey

#### 1. Becoming a Tasker
```
Sign Up ‚Üí Complete Tasker Profile ‚Üí Upload ID Documents ‚Üí 
Set Experience Level ‚Üí Add Bio ‚Üí Define Service Areas ‚Üí 
Set Availability ‚Üí Submit for Verification ‚Üí Profile Under Review ‚Üí 
Verification Approved ‚Üí Start Offering Services
```

#### 2. Offering Services
```
Create Service ‚Üí Add Description & Pricing ‚Üí Upload Portfolio Images ‚Üí 
Set Service Area ‚Üí Set Availability ‚Üí Submit for Review ‚Üí 
Service Approved ‚Üí Service Live ‚Üí Receive Booking Requests ‚Üí 
Accept/Decline Bookings ‚Üí Complete Services ‚Üí Receive Payments
```

#### 3. Applying to Jobs
```
Browse Jobs ‚Üí Filter by Preferences ‚Üí View Job Details ‚Üí 
Submit Application (with proposal & pricing) ‚Üí 
Optional: Purchase Premium Application ‚Üí 
Wait for Customer Response ‚Üí Application Accepted ‚Üí 
Job Assigned ‚Üí Complete Job ‚Üí Receive Payment
```

### Payment Process

#### 1. Service Booking Payment
```
Customer Books Service ‚Üí Payment Authorized ‚Üí Payment Held in Escrow ‚Üí 
Service Scheduled ‚Üí Service Completed ‚Üí Customer Confirms ‚Üí 
Payment Released to Tasker ‚Üí Platform Fee Deducted
```

#### 2. Job Payment
```
Job Assigned ‚Üí Payment Authorized ‚Üí Payment Held in Escrow ‚Üí 
Job Completed ‚Üí Customer Confirms ‚Üí Payment Released to Tasker ‚Üí 
Platform Fee Deducted
```

#### 3. Refund Process
```
Dispute Raised ‚Üí Platform Review ‚Üí Resolution Decision ‚Üí 
If Refund Approved ‚Üí Payment Refunded to Customer
```
